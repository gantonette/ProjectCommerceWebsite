Is your produce ethically sourced?

Our produce are sourced from local Wellington farms who ensure that the process of obtaining our produce is done in a way that is responsible and ensures the use of sustainable methods.

Do you sell 100% organic produce?

At Greendew Village our products are 100% organic!

How do I contact you if I don't receive my order?

If you have placed an order with us and have not received it within 24 hours since purchase, please call us on 0800-Greendew-Village ASAP.

Do you use pesticides on your produce?

Our products do not contain any pesticides.

Will there be more produce options?

The team at Greendew Valley are in the process of expanding our product range with numerous types of vegetables and fruits! We will announce when the release of our products will be available for the general public!

Are refunds available?

In rare circumstances where Greendew Village have failed to deliver your produce within 24 hours, a full refund will be made available. Please contact us on garden@greendewvillage.com.

How much is shipping?

Shipping cost $5.00 across Wellington. Unfortunately, we have not yet expanded our services outside of Wellington yet. Products purchased after 4:00pm will be shipped the following day within 24 hours.

What is your mission?

At Greendew Village we seek to promote healthy eating of fresh local fruit and vege produce. Buy local and eat fresh Welly!

How long is shipping?

Shipping should not take any longer than 24 hours after initial purchase!

How can I give a review or provide some criticism?

If you feel Greendew Village has not met your standards, please contact us at garden@greendewvillage.com. or call us on 0800 Greendew Village.

What are your contact details?

To contact us for any enquiries, send us an email at garden@greendewvillage.com. or call us on 0800 Greendew Village. You can find us on Facebook at @GreendewVillage or on Instagram at @GreendewVillage
